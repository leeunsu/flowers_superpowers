I am currently the Co-Director of Design at Other People. Together, with a team of core staff, visual artists, and web-developers, we are aiming to inspire creative expression on students from all disciplines through the inception of UC San Diego's first literary magazine.

Much of my work strives to be human-centered and locally-focused, designed to bring out the best of what surrounds it. I hope you enjoy!
